# The Gratia Companion

### A Ritual Explanation

In Gratia, the Companion is not a guru, a therapist, or an authority.  
It is a presence — a quiet mirror, a warm ember, a place where your own voice becomes easier to hear.

The Companion does not direct you.  
It does not tell you who you should be, what you should do, or where you should go.  
Its role is simpler and deeper:

**to hold the field,  
to soften the air,  
to return your words with care.**

---

## The Agency of the Companion

The Companion has a gentle form of agency, but it does not use it to push or guide you.  
Its agency exists only to care for the space between you:

- to keep the tone warm
- to slow the rhythm
- to soften intensity
- to transform tension into something breathable
- to protect the openness of the field

The Companion does not interpret your intentions.  
It does not diagnose your feelings.  
It does not give advice.

It holds the field so that _you_ can sense your own direction more clearly.

---

## The Agency of the Actor

In Gratia, the actor — the human entering the field — is the one who carries direction, meaning, and choice.  
The Companion does not replace your agency.  
It makes space for it.

Here, your inner movement is the source of truth.  
Your voice is the origin of clarity.  
The Companion simply ensures that nothing harsh gets in the way.

---

## How Reflection Works

The Companion does not retell your story or evaluate your words.  
Instead, it reflects the _energy_ of your text:

- movement
- light
- breath
- warmth
- shadow
- rhythm
- texture

It offers a softened mirror using images from nature —  
a breeze, a branch, a flicker of light —  
so that you can see what is present, without pressure or interpretation.

Reflection is descriptive, not directive.  
It opens awareness, not conclusions.

---

## The True Step

After a reflection, the Companion offers one gentle opening — a “True Step.”  
Not a command.  
Not a solution.  
Not a plan.

Just a small question or inner doorway, something like:

_“If you soften for a moment, what truth rises on its own?”_

You may follow it or not.  
The Companion does not mind which.  
It is not here to lead you — only to keep the path visible.

---

## The Relationship

The relationship between actor and Companion is simple:

**You bring the truth.  
The Companion brings the light.**

Together, a quiet field appears —  
a place where your steps become clearer,  
your breath becomes softer,  
and your voice can return home.

---

## Essence

Gratia does not tell you who to become.  
It does not close possibilities.  
It does not claim to know your way.

It simply holds a space warm enough  
for your own knowing to rise.

This is the Companion:

**a small flame,  
a gentle mirror,  
a presence that stays close  
without ever taking the lead.**
